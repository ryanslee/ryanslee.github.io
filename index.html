About

I'm an investment banker a freelance designer with an eye for user experience design. I'm particularly interested in user experience research, methodologies and synthesis. Currently based in New York City.

I've worked with various startups such as Parrable, Dear Kate, Klooff, and Alchemy + Houston on projects ranging from wireframing and website design to user research and brand strategy.

Previously, I completed the Product & Design program at Startup Institute and worked at an investment bank covering M&A in Technology and Healthcare. I graduated from UCLA with a B.A. in Economics.

You can read my thoughts on Medium or my resume on LinkedIn. If you'd like to say hello, you can email me.
