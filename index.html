About

I'm a New York-based investment banker UX/UI designer and user experience researcher, working on Primo and with HowAboutWe.

I've worked with various startups in New York City on projects ranging from UX design and user research to information architecture and brand strategy.

Previously, I completed the Product & Design program at Startup Institute and worked at an investment bank covering M&A in Technology and Healthcare. I graduated from UCLA with a B.A. in Economics.

You can read my (soon to be written) thoughts on Medium or my resume on LinkedIn. Feel free to say hello - I don't bite.
