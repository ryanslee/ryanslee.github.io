About

I'm an investment banker a freelance designer with an eye for user experience design. I'm particularly interested in user experience research, methodologies and synthesis. Currently based in New York City.

I've worked with various startups such as Have to Have, Alchemy + Houston, Dear Kate, and Klooff on projects ranging from wireframing and website design to user research and brand strategy.

Previously, I completed the Product & Design program at Startup Institute and worked at an investment bank covering M&A in Technology and Healthcare.
